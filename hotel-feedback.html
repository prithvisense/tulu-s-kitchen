Welcome to TULU's Kitchen

This is a simple hero unit, a simple jumbotron-style component for calling extra attention to featured content or information.

TULU'S Kitchen with its prime location in the center of Baksara, right in the heart of Santragachi with numerous dishes of great cuisine is a best choice for good vegetarian food and ambiance. TULU'S Kitchen is about experiencing a feast, a celebration along with family and friends in a cozy modern setting. A place where authentic Indian pure vegetarian cuisine and dishes from north and south of India are prepared using high quality fresh ingredients and served in a fusion of Indian and Thai hospitality. TULU'S Kitchen first opened its doors and offered mouth-watering Indian Vegetarian delicacies on Sept 10, 2002. We are frequented by vegans and vegetarians around the world, locals, tourists and resident international community in India. TULU'S Kitchen IS A 100% PURE INDIAN VEGETARIAN RESTAURANT .
